Design Principles

Visual Hierarchy

Nike

Nike.com

Nike's website does a great job using visual hierarchy by displaying what is important right at the top where the viewer first sees the information. In this case it displays current sales and all information for the consumer at the top.


White Space

YouTube

YouTube.com

Youtube's website has a good example of proper white space usage(or black space in this case). Things are not cluttered on top of each other and there is proper padding between each element.


PARC: Contrast

Zillow

Zillow.com

Zillow's website has great examples of having good contrast. There is good contrast between the font color and the images they are placed next to and the background colors of the website as well.
