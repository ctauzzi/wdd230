Ciao! I am Jennifer of Jennifiore Flowers…and I love flowers. Growing up in upstate New York, I fell in love with nature and trees and all forms of greenery, but my love for florals didn’t fully come about until I got a job working at a tree nursery in Southeastern Idaho. Getting to tend to and work with plants and flowers everyday got me fixated on what I now want to do for the rest of my life! After that I started studying horticulture and floral design and haven’t looked back. I’ve taken on several jobs working at floral shops and modern plant design studios to hone my skills and discover new passions.

After marrying into an Italian family, I try to incorporate the language and culture more into my life, even into my blossoming business’s name. In Italian, the word fiore means ‘flower’. I like to think that I am working every day to continue to bloom, blossom, and abound. My goal is to help create the perfect wedding or event so that you can see your dreams BLOOM.
