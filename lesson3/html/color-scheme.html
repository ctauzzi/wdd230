Color Scheme

Main

The main background color will be #90ee90 (light green).


Headings and paragraphs

Headings and paragraphs will be #000000 (black).


Navigation

The navigation bar will have the background color of #adde86 (light blue) and with a text color of #00008b (dark blue).


Footer

The footer will have a background color of #adde86 (light blue) and a text color of #00008b (dark blue).
